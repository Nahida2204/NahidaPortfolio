I am a final-year BSc Computer Science with Systems Engineering student at Middlesex University Mauritius, graduating in July 2026. My academic work is complemented by practical experience across industry placements, where I have built and deployed systems that are actively used within real business environments.

During my traineeship at Leal & Co Ltd, I worked within a structured problem-solving methodology — assess, design, construct, implement, and review — that shaped how I approach technical challenges. Applying this framework, I identified an operational bottleneck at Leal Bodyshop and developed it into my capstone project: an AI-powered Progressive Web Application using computer vision to automate vehicle damage assessment, a process previously done entirely by hand.

My core interests are in artificial intelligence, intelligent business systems, and process automation — specifically how these technologies can be applied practically to improve real organisational workflows.
